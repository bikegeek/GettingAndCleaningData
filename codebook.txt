Tidy data description:

A tidy.txt file is created by the run_analysis.R script applied to the above Samsung data.
The columns are: subject, activity, measurement, measurment average value.

The subject is the number assigned to the subject participating in the study.  There were
30 people involved in the study, and the subject numbers range from 1-30.

The activity is a description of the activity that the subject was engaged, and can be any one of the following: walking, walking upstairs, walking downstairs, sitting, standing, or laying.
